STEP-BY-STEP: ADD NEW LOAN (PowerShell)
======================================

1. Drag your documents into new_uploads/
   • Put your PDF here: debt-empire\loans\new_uploads\
   • Example: bjmagnarepay1.pdf

2. Open PowerShell and run:
   cd "C:\Users\MYPC\Desktop\debt-arbitrage\debt-empire"
   py loan_verifier.py

3. In the verifier:
   • Enter choice [1-4]: 1   (Verify new loan)
   • Enter choice [1-4]: 1   (Parse Bajaj PDF)
   • PDF path: bjmagnarepay1.pdf   (or your filename)
   • Complete the wizard → Y to save

4. Regenerate dashboard:
   py empire.py

5. Open dashboard and print/email:
   start dashboard.html
   → Ctrl+P → Save as PDF → Email

OR run the all-in-one script:
   .\ADD_NEW_LOAN.ps1
   (Opens this folder, then runs verifier → empire → dashboard)
